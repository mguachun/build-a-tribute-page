Danny DeVito

Actor, Comedian, and Filmmaker

Here's a time line of Danny DeVito facts!:

  • Danny Devito has been in, quoting the all knowing Google, "at least 82 movies." 82!
  • Devito first became famous for his role as taxi dispatcher Louie De Palma in the 1980's sitcom Taxi.
  • He once was a voice over in My Little Pony.
  • His Net worth is $70 million.
  • Before diving into acting, Devito worked at his sister's hair salon for 18 months.
  • Danny Devito has three children with his wife Rhea Perlman.
  • In the 2016 election Devito endorsed democratic socialist Bernie Sanders.
  • Danny Devito has been credited with saving the show It's Always Sunny In Philadelphia. The show's first season had such low ratings that FX mandated they get a "name" actor. Devito stepped into the role of Frank Reynolds and ratings quickly rose.
